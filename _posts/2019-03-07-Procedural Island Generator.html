My first procedural generation project is a simple island generator

The island starts out 1x1 land tile. Land tiles are marked with the green block. All adjacent tiles next to the block are considered candidates for expansion. The candidates are marked with the black block.

Every round, half the number of tile locations candidates get randomly chosen. The chosen ones get to become land tiles. The new tiles do a quick surrounding search and place new potential candidate tiles. Repeat until satisfied!

Controls:

Left click: Run a round of tile generation
Right click: Fix unchosen candidates inside island
Space bar: Reset generation

The first number on the top corner indicates whether a click is registered or not
The second number on the top corner indicates the amount of possible candidates for land tiles to generate on.
