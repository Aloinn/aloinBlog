Controls

Left arrow: Move left
Right arrow: Move right
Shift: New game

AI Explained

How the AI for my version of pong works is that I measure the cycles it will take before the ball reaches the other paddle. Afterwards, the # of cycles is used to calculate how much horizontal distance the ball will cover in total.

If the ball's horizontal speed + ball's x coorinate is still in canvas
The AI paddle will go to the position of horizontal speed + ball's x coordinate

OTHERWISE
If the ball is going to bounce off a wall. The horizontal distance is divided by canvas width.

If the floored value is EVEN, (Will bounce off walls odd number of times)
it will meet the AI at a distance from the side the ball is currently moving away from

If the floored value is ODD, (Will bounce off walls even number of times
it will meet the AI at a distance from the side the ball is currently moving towards

The said distance is the remainder after dividing the total distance travelled by the canvas width.
